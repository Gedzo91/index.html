Managing Your Experience

If someone's behavior is bothering you:

  • Click the 🔇 button in the bottom left to see all players
  • Use the mute button next to a player's name to hide them
  • Unmute them anytime to see them again

While advanced moderation features are work-in-progress, please use the mute button if someone is bothering you. For serious issues, take a screenshot and report in the Stupid-Looking Games Discord: discord.gg/6UaSAF6qMr
